About Amy's Artistry

Amy's art comes in many mediums. Her favorite being acrylic paints. Her paintings can vary from realsitic to abstract, always full of color. She takes her inspiration from nature and paints as a form of mediation and escape from reality to let her mind wander free and unhindered.

Meet the Artist

Hi! My name is Amy. I've been an artist most of my life. Since I was a young child, I have loved anything and everything that is Art. I am completly and utterly enthralled with Art; as a thought then a concept to fruitation of a piece. The artisitc process is compelling and fascinating.

I began my artistic journey as most children doing, playing in the mud. I must have been doing pretty well sculpting creations out of mud becuase my family began buying me clay to sculpt with. Once I was in school and learned how to use a pencil correctly, I began drawing. I drew for hours, it very quickly became my favorite activity. I moved onto colored pencils and pens and eventually found paints.

Acrylic paints are my favorite medium. Easy, versatile and fun to use. It has become a form of self expression and escapism from the mundane day to day.
